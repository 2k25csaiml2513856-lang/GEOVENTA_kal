How It Works

The Scoring Engine

A transparent, research-backed methodology combining geospatial analysis and multi-criteria decision making.

01
📍

Candidate Zone Generation

The engine generates candidate zones around your target city based on your business type and radius preference, using real geospatial offsets or live geocoding when an API key is active.

02
👥

Population & Demand Analysis

Census-style population density and spending power data are mapped to each zone. High-density, high-income areas receive a demand multiplier appropriate to the business type.

03
🏪

Competitor Cluster Detection

The number of existing businesses in the same sector is counted per zone using Google Places API (or synthetic proxies). Market saturation reduces a zone's opportunity score.

04
💰

Land Valuation & Budget Fit

Estimated land or lease costs are compared against your monthly budget. Zones within budget score higher on the cost-efficiency axis, while overbudget zones are flagged.

05
🤖

MCDA Weighted Scoring

All factors are normalized to [0,1] and combined using user-defined weights via MCDA. A composite score (0–100) is assigned to each zone and zones are ranked automatically.

06
📈

Land Price Forecasting

A compound growth model with zone-specific growth rate, demand pressure, and infrastructure score projects land valuation 1–10 years into the future with a confidence band.
